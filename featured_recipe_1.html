Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Delicious and comforting homemade chicken noodle soup

About This Recipe

Chicken noodle soup is a warm, comforting dish perfect for chilly days or when you're feeling under the weather. This homemade version is packed with tender chicken, vegetables, and hearty noodles, all simmered in a flavorful broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 6 cups (1.5L) chicken broth
  • 1 Â½ cups egg noodles (or any noodles of your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: Fresh parsley or dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic. Cook for about 3-4 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the carrots and celery to the pot, and cook for another 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Add the dried thyme and parsley. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. While the broth is simmering, add the chicken breasts or thighs. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it with two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  6. Add the egg noodles and cook according to the package directions (about 7-8 minutes).
  7. Once the noodles are cooked, taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or dill, if desired.
