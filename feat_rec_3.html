Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and follow the package instructions.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Fill each taco shell with the cooked meat.
  5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa as desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
