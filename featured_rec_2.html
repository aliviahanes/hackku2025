Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (cold, cubed)
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for strawberries)
  • 1 cup whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in milk and vanilla until just combined.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. While the shortcakes bake, mix strawberries with sugar and let sit for 10 minutes.
  7. Once shortcakes are cool, slice them in half and layer with strawberries and whipped cream.
  8. Top with more whipped cream and a strawberry. Enjoy! ‚ù§Ô∏è
